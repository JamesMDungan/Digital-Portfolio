The Ludicrous project is as the name suggests Ludicrous. The genius minds of James Dungan (CS - ii), Soohan Cho (CS - ii), Robbie Hutchings (CS- ii), Vivian Medina (CS - ii) and the mediocre mind of Jacob Fesseha (CS - ii). Bring you our own twist on the simulator genre of games. In this game you will control a goose and have free reign to do whatever you wish in this crazy world we've created. Whether its shoot sharks out of your mouth of summon hordes of fish imagination is simply the limit. Using the Roblox Studio gine we can create crazy cool mechanics and features for the game and make the time when you play it truly Ludicrous.
